SLEEP MEDITATION - 20 MINUTES
Evidence-Based Techniques: Extended Body Scan + Sleep-Inducing Breathing + Deep Visualization

[0:00] Welcome to this 20-minute sleep meditation. Settle into bed, arranging your body in the most comfortable position for sleep.

[0:25] Close your eyes and let them remain closed.

[0:40] Begin with a few deep, calming breaths. Breathe in through your nose... and out through your mouth with a gentle sigh.

[1:00] Again, breathe in deeply... and sigh it out, releasing the day.

[1:20] One more time... breathe in... and let everything go.

[1:40] Now allow your breath to find its own natural, slow rhythm. Easy breathing. Effortless.

[2:05] With each exhale, feel yourself sinking a little deeper into the bed. Heavier. More relaxed.

[PAUSE 20 seconds]

[2:55] Let all the tiny muscles in your feet release and relax. Feel them becoming heavy.

[3:20] Notice your ankles... so many small movements they make during the day. Now they can be completely still and at rest.

[3:45] Bring awareness to your lower legs... your shins... your calves. Feel them softening, releasing, becoming heavy and relaxed.

[4:15] Notice your knees. Let them unlock and soften completely.

[4:35] Bring attention to your thighs. These large, strong muscles have carried you all day. Now they can completely let go. Feel them sinking down, heavy and relaxed.

[5:05] Notice your hips and buttocks. Release any gripping. Let this whole area soften and spread across the bed.

[5:35] Bring awareness to your lower belly. With each breath, feel it gently rising and falling. So peaceful. So calm.

[6:05] Notice your middle back and sides. Let them spread wide, supported by the bed beneath you.

[6:30] Bring attention to your upper belly and lower ribs. Feel them moving gently with your breath. Easy. Natural.

[7:00] Notice your chest and upper back. Feel your breath moving in this area like gentle waves. Slow. Soothing.

[7:30] Bring awareness to your shoulders. Often we carry so much tension here. With your next exhale, let your shoulders drop heavily. Release. Let go.

[8:05] Feel the space this creates in your neck. More openness. More ease.

[8:25] Notice your upper arms... so heavy now... your elbows soft... your forearms releasing.

[8:50] Bring attention to your wrists and hands. Let your hands rest completely open and soft. Nothing to hold. Nothing to do.

[9:20] Notice your fingers, all the way to the tips. Let them be completely relaxed and still.

[9:45] Bring awareness to your neck. Let it be long and free, your head heavy on the pillow.

[10:10] Notice your throat. Soft and open. Breathing easily.

[10:30] Bring attention to your jaw. One of the most common places we hold tension. Let your jaw hang loose. Teeth slightly apart. Tongue soft in your mouth.

[11:00] Notice your lips... barely touching or gently parted. Your cheeks, heavy and soft.

[11:25] Bring awareness to your nose and sinuses. Air flowing easily in and out.

[11:45] Notice your eyes, closed and heavy. Let them sink back into their sockets. No effort. Just rest.

[12:10] Feel your eyebrows and forehead. Smooth out any lines. Let your whole forehead be wide and soft.

[12:35] Notice your temples... your ears... the sides of your head. All relaxed.

[12:55] Bring awareness to your scalp and the crown of your head. Let any last bits of tension melt away.

[13:20] Your entire body is now deeply, completely relaxed. Heavy. Still. At peace.

[16:45] The bed is perfectly made with the softest blankets and pillows.

[17:05] You settle into this bed... it conforms perfectly to your body... supporting you completely.

[17:30] You're surrounded by warmth and comfort. Safe. Protected. Peaceful.

[17:55] Through the window, you see the night sky. Stars twinkling. The moon glowing softly.

[18:20] Everything is quiet. Still. Perfect for sleep.
